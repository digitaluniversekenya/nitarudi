Privacy Policy — NitaRudi
Last updated: April 2026

NitaRudi is a customer leads tracking app built for Kenyan sellers.

DATA WE COLLECT
NitaRudi does not collect, transmit, or store any data on external servers. All information you enter — including customer contact
details, lead notes, shop name, and phone number — is stored exclusively on your device using your phone's local storage.

We do not have access to your data. We cannot see your leads, your customer information, or your sales activity.

DATA YOU ENTER
The app stores the following information locally on your device:

Your shop name and phone number
Customer names, phone numbers, and social media handles
Product descriptions and notes
Follow-up dates and lead status
This data never leaves your device and is not shared with any third party.

THIRD-PARTY SERVICES
When you tap the follow-up button, NitaRudi opens third-party apps (WhatsApp, Instagram, TikTok, Facebook) on your device.
These apps have their own privacy policies. NitaRudi does not share any data with these platforms — it simply opens them.

DATA DELETION
You can delete all your data at any time by going to Settings → Reset profile inside the app, or by uninstalling the app from your device.

CHILDREN
NitaRudi is not intended for use by children under the age of 13.

CHANGES TO THIS POLICY
We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app's Play Store listing.

CONTACT
If you have any questions about this privacy policy, contact us at: digitaluniversekenya@gmail.com

SELLER RESPONSIBILITY AND DATA COMPLIANCE

NitaRudi is a tool that enables sellers to manage customer follow-ups. As a seller using this app, you are the data controller of any customer information you enter, including names, phone numbers, and social media handles.

By using NitaRudi, you agree that:

You are responsible for ensuring your use of customer data complies with the Kenya Data Protection Act 2019 and any other applicable
laws and regulations.

You will only store details of customers who voluntarily contacted you and provided their information in the context of a genuine
business enquiry.

You will use customer contact information solely for the purpose of following up on business enquiries and not for unsolicited marketing,
harassment, or any other purpose.

You will honour any request from a customer to stop contacting them or to delete their information from your records.

You understand that NitaRudi limits follow-up reminders to a maximum of 2 per customer to help protect customer privacy, but you remain
responsible for all direct contact you make outside of the app.

NitaRudi Limited does not accept liability for any breach of data protection laws arising from a seller's use of the app. If you are
unsure of your obligations under the Kenya Data Protection Act 2019, we recommend you seek independent legal advice.
